# Technology research
Raw technology research material and its derived insights.

## Structure

- `Evidence/` - technical notes, benchmarks, references, experiments, and other source material
- `Insights/` - synthesized technology insights derived from the evidence

## Usage

- Add new source material to `Evidence/`
- Capture reusable synthesis in `Insights/`
- Promote durable knowledge into `Knowledge/` when it becomes part of the curated vault

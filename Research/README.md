# Research

This folder holds raw research material and intermediate synthesis before it is curated into `Knowledge/`.

## Structure

- `Customer/` - customer discovery research
- `Market/` - market research and competitive or category scans
- `Technology/` - technical research, references, and experiments
- `Signals/` - observations and weak signals that may become relevant later

Each major research area may use this pattern:

- `Evidence/` - raw source material such as notes, transcripts, reports, datasets, references, or experiments
- `Insights/` - synthesized takeaways derived from the evidence

## Usage

- Store source material in the appropriate research area under `Evidence/`
- Capture intermediate synthesis in `Insights/`
- Promote durable concepts, strategy, decisions, metrics, and playbooks into `Knowledge/` when they are stable enough to become part of the curated vault

## Rule of thumb

- `Research/` is exploratory and source-oriented
- `Knowledge/` is curated and durable

Treat `Research/` as read-only source material unless a task explicitly calls for editing it.
